Play, Learn

and get outdoors

Squirrels is Scouting's newest section and are young people aged 4 to 6.

Squirrels can achieve anything they set their minds to – and they have lots of fun along the way. The one hour sessions will be a brilliant opportunity for young people to take their first step into being part of Scouts, and they’ll help everyone get ready for the adventure of school too. Sessions will be packed with outdoors activities, fun, games, badges, laughter, and so much more to help our youngest members learn the skills they need for life. Of course, none of it would be possible without the support of the amazing leaders who plan and deliver such an inspiring programme.

We don't have any active Dreys in our district but if you'd like to express your interest and join the waiting list, get in touch by emailing adcbeavers@longeatonscouts.org.uk
