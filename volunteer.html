Volunteer

Share your skills,

gain new ones

You don't need to be Bear Grylls to be a volunteer. We need tea makers, activity planners, tidy-uppers and helpers for as little or as much time as you can spare. If you're a students who want to boost their CV, or a parent who wants to spend more time together as a family, you could lend a hand. And Scouting's award-winning training scheme for volunteers means that adults get as much from Scouting as our young people.

If you're looking to help out in the district, get in touch by emailing secretary@longeatonscouts.org.uk
