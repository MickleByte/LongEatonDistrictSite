Trent Lock Activity Centre

Trent Lock activity center is on the banks of the River Trent where people of all ages take part in a range of activites both on and off the water. Trent Lock is dedicated to giving young people an opportunity to experience canoeing, sailing, raft building and other activities with plenty of fun thrown in!

Activities

Being on the banks of the Trent, the site is able to offer a range of water activites including Canoeing, Kayaking, Rafting and Sailing. Training courses

Where to find us

The campsite is near J24 of the M1, take the B6540 towards Long Eaton and then follow the brown 'Trent Lock' signs down Lock Lane. At the end of the lane is a free car park where you can leave your car.

To get to the campsite from the car park head past the pub and up onto the flood bank, you'll see the activity centre on the other side of the river. Turn right and head along the river to the concrete pontoon where you'll be picked up by our ferry boat
