Activity Name

Archery can be provided at the centre and is one of our popular activities. As an adventurous activity and sport, it is delivered qualified Archery GB instructors, making it safe and enjoyable. Small groups of Scouts and Guides can take turns on the range, initially tutored on a one-to-one basis to achieve a worthwhile level of competence.
