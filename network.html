Be the best you can be

Your Scout experience doesn’t need to stop when you turn 18. Whether you’re completely new to Scouts or have been with us since you were 5 years old it’s all about working together to build new skills, seek out new experiences and make a difference in the world. Long Eaton Network is based at the Sea Scout hut on Tamworth Rd and meets fortnightly, both at the hut and out and about, doing a variety of activities centred around the Community, International and Adventure themes. For a flavour, here's some of what we've been up to this year:

  • Making wine from scratch
  • Paddleboarding
  • Working on a classic car
  • Completing the Yorkshire Three Peaks Challenge
  • Indoor Bouldering

The Network group also gives an opportunity to do top awards like the Explorer Belt, Queenscout and Gold Duke of Edinburgh awards.

To get started in Network, send us an email at info@erewashscouts.org.uk
