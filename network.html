Be the best you can be

Your Scout experience doesn’t need to stop when you turn 18. Whether you’re completely new to Scouts or have been with us since you were 5 years old it’s all about working together to build new skills, seek out new experiences and make a difference in the world

Network is a bit different from the other sections in Scouting and groups don’t necessarily meet every week. That doesn't mean that there isn't plenty to get stuck into. To get started in Network, send us an email at network@longeatonscouts.org.uk
